Financial Literacy Basics

Financial literacy is your superpower! Learn about income, expenses, credit, and investments to make informed decisions. Did you know teens who understand money early are 50% more likely to build wealth by adulthood?

Fun Fact: Start with apps like Khan Academy's finance courses – they're free and interactive!

Saving Strategies for Teens

Saving isn't just about stashing cash, it's about building habits. Aim to save 10-20% of your allowance or earnings. Use high-yield savings accounts for better interest.

Tip: Automate transfers to a savings app like Acorns – watch your money grow like a tree!

Mastering Budgeting

Budgeting tracks where your money goes. List income, subtract expenses, and allocate for needs, wants, and savings. Tools like Mint or YNAB make it easy.

Interactive: Create a pie chart of your spending – visualize and cut unnecessary costs!

Entrepreneurship Ideas

Turn hobbies into hustles! Start a Kwek-Kwek stand, sell handmade crafts online, or freelance graphic design. Learn from icons like Oprah or Elon Musk.

Challenge: Brainstorm one business idea today and research it – your future empire starts here!
